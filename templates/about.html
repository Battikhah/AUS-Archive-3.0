About AUS Archive

Learn about our mission, our team, and how we're helping students succeed

Our Mission

AUS Archive is a student-led initiative with a clear mission: to provide a centralized repository of academic resources for students at American University of Sharjah. We believe that knowledge should be shared and accessible to all students.

Community Collaboration

We foster a collaborative environment where students can share resources and help each other succeed academically.

Academic Excellence

Our platform provides access to quality study materials that help students achieve their academic goals and excel in their courses.

Resource Preservation

We preserve valuable academic resources and make them available to future generations of students.

Our Story

2023

The Beginning

AUS Archive was founded by a group of students who recognized the need for a centralized platform to share academic resources. What started as a small project quickly gained traction among the student body.

2024

Growth and Development

The platform expanded to include more courses and resource types. We implemented user feedback and improved the user experience, making it easier for students to find and share valuable materials.

2025

Looking Forward

Our vision for the future includes expanding our resource library, enhancing the platform's features, and exploring new ways to support the academic success of AUS students.

How You Can Contribute

AUS Archive thrives on community contributions. Here's how you can help:

  • Share Resources: Upload your class notes, past exams, and study guides to help other students.
  • Provide Feedback: Help us improve by sharing your suggestions and reporting any issues.
  • Spread the Word: Tell your classmates about AUS Archive and encourage them to contribute.
